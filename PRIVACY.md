# Privacy Policy for TabFade

**Last updated:** April 4, 2026

## Data Collection

TabFade does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

- **Local storage:** Tab activity timestamps and closed tab history
- **Sync storage:** User preferences (expiry time, whitelist domains, toggle states)

This data never leaves your browser.

## Third-Party Services

TabFade does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

## Permissions

TabFade requests browser permissions solely to provide its core functionality (auto-closing inactive tabs). See the Chrome Web Store listing for detailed permission justifications.
